Saint Paul Stylee: A Three Eleven Tribute

We are 5 guys that grew up listening to 311 and just so happen to play the right mix of instruments to perform the songs that we love.

Our accurate renditions of 311 songs include the singles, as well as deeper tracks spanning the band’s entire career. You’ll hear Down, Amber, and Beautiful Disaster as well as Grassroots, Hostile Apostle and Welcome. Our goal is to capture the positivity of 311’s music and bring it to each show with stellar musicianship and energetic stage presence.

Get in Touch

We are actively looking to meet other 90s-2000s tribute acts and cover bands, Hip-hop groups, or reggae bands looking to share shows. Our current setlist allows us to split a night with one other band and we’d happily partner with 2 or 3 other groups to provide a more dynamic evening of music.

Checkout the promo video or demo tracks and if you are interested in gigging with us, email us at booking@saintpaulstylee.com and lets set something up.
